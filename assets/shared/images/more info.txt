If you're curious on what happens after Error 404, read this.

After Error 404, Dracobot crashes offscreen and becomes unresponsive.
He became unresponsive for 5 minutes to later regain control of himself again.
Spirit returned to the disc he was in during the time Dracobot was unresponsive.
Dracobot immediately removed the game from him and put it inside a container and gave it to girlfriend.

To put it simply, Dracobot is okay after the events of Error 404 and Spirit is back inside of the game he was trapped in.
